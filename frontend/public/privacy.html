Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your information.

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Introduction

K-12 Learning Portal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our students, parents, educators, and all users of our educational platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our learning portal and related services.

We comply with all applicable federal and state laws regarding student privacy, including the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the Student Online Personal Information Protection Act (SOPIPA).

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our educational services:

  • Student Information: Name, grade level, school, student ID, academic records, and learning progress data
  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password
  • Usage Data: Course completion, assessment scores, time spent on activities, and learning preferences
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security purposes
  • Parent/Guardian Information: Contact details for authorized communications regarding student progress

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information exclusively for educational purposes:

  • Providing personalized learning experiences and adaptive curriculum
  • Tracking academic progress and generating progress reports
  • Communicating with parents and educators about student achievement
  • Improving our educational content and platform functionality
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access
  • Complying with legal requirements and educational regulations

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access to student data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted
  • Multi-factor authentication is available for all accounts
  • Automatic session timeouts protect unattended devices

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade student information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With Schools: We share student progress data with authorized school administrators and teachers
  • With Parents/Guardians: Parents have access to their child's academic information
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help operate our platform, bound by strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety

COPPA Compliance

For users under 13 years of age, we comply with COPPA requirements:

  • Parental consent is obtained before collecting personal information from children
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information upon request
  • We collect only information necessary for educational purposes
  • We do not condition participation on disclosure of more information than necessary

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications

Data Retention

We retain student educational records for the duration of enrollment plus seven years, in accordance with educational record-keeping requirements. Non-essential data is deleted after 30 days of account inactivity.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or track students for marketing purposes. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@k12portal.edu
  • Phone: 1-800-555-LEARN (option 3)
  • Mail: K-12 Learning Portal, Privacy Office, 123 Education Way, Learning City, ED 12345

For FERPA-related inquiries, you may also contact the U.S. Department of Education.
