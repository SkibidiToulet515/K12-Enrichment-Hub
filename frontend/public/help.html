Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
What devices are supported?
Our platform works on all modern devices including Windows PCs, Mac computers, Chromebooks, iPads, Android tablets, and smartphones. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.
How can parents monitor their child's progress?
Parents with linked accounts can access a dedicated parent dashboard showing their child's course progress, grades, attendance, and recent activity. Weekly progress reports are also sent via email. Contact your school administrator to set up parent access.
Is my child's data safe and private?
Absolutely. We comply with FERPA, COPPA, and all applicable student privacy laws. Student data is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and only shared with authorized school personnel. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
What should I do if a video won't play?
First, try refreshing the page and clearing your browser cache. Ensure you have a stable internet connection (we recommend at least 5 Mbps). If problems persist, try a different browser or disable any ad-blocking extensions that might interfere with video playback.
How do I contact my teacher?
You can message your teacher directly through the platform's messaging system. Go to your course page and click "Message Instructor." Teachers typically respond within one business day. For urgent matters, contact your school office directly.
